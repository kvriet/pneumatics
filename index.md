This website contains information on how to design and build pneumatically actuated projects. It is based on questions I commonly get from students. I decided to answer them all in one place, so you can easily find the reference material.

_Are you a TU/e student and your question isn't answered here? Send me a message on the TU/e Teams and I'll try to help you._

Topics:
1. Air Circuits: how does air flow through a system?
1. Pneumatic Interfaces and Devices: what to keep in mind when designing inflatables?
1. AirSystem: how can I use the AirSystem to power my prototypes?
1. AirSystem ESP32: how can I use the AirSystem ESP32 to power my prototypes?
1. Electronics: how can I design and build my own pump/valve/pressure sensor system?
